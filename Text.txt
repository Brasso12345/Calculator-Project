       a arithmetic
            a From 1st term to nth term
            b Find the nth term
            c Sum of all terms
            d Find the common difference
            e Arithmetic Mean
       b geometric
            a From 1st term to nth term
            b Find the nth term
            c Sum of all terms
            d Find the common ratio
            e Find the Geometric Mean
       c fibonacci
            a From 0th term to nth term
            b From xth term to yth term
            c Find the nth term
            d Find the Summation of terms from f0 to fn
            e Find the Summation of terms from fx to fy
